Rules

Certain rules of this game are based on this rules sheet by Hasbro games.

How to win

Get as many points as possible by rolling five dices and getting certain combinations of dice.

Gameplay

In each turn a player may throw the dices up to three times. After each throw, a player doesn't have to roll all five dices. Player can choose to save as many dices as they wish to the side and only throw the ones that don't have the numbers they're trying to get. For example, a player rolls and gets 1, 2, 2, 3, 6. The player decides to try and go for a large straight 1,2,3,4,5. Setting aside 1, 2, 3 and roll again hoping to get 4 and 5.

Player click on the dice the wish to keep. It will be grayed out and saved the next time they roll. If you decide to no longer keep the dice, you can click the dice again to unsave.

Upper section

  • Ones: Get as many ones as possible
  • Twos: Get as many twos as possible
  • Threes: Get as many threes as possible
  • Fours: Get as many fours as possible
  • Fives: Get as many fives as possible
  • Sixes: Get as many sixes as possible

For the upper section combinations listed above, the score for each of them is the sum of dice of the same kind. Example, if player roll 1,1,1,3,6 and player choose Ones, player will get 3 points, because there are 3 of Ones. The sum of all the combinations above is calculated and if it is 63 or more, the player will get a bonus on 35 points.

Lower section

  • Three of a kind: Get three dices with the same number. Scores 12 points.
  • Four of a kind: Get four dices with the same number. Scores 27 points.
  • Full house: Get three of a kind and a pair, example 2,2,3,3,3 or 5,5,1,1,1. Scores 25 points.
  • Small straight: Get four sequential dice, 1,2,3,4, or 2,3,4,5 or 3,4,5,6. Scores 30 points.
  • Large straight: Get five sequential dice, 1,2,3,4,5 or 2,3,4,5,6. Scores 40 points.
  • Chance: The score is the sum of all current dices.
  • Yahtzee: Five of a kind. Scores 50 points.

You cannot get multiple yahtzees in this game.
